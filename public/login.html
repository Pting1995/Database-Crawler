About Database Crawler:

Welcome to Database Crawler! This game is in early Alpha, but please play it and have fun! The game is a text-based RPG. When you create an account you can create a character and embark on an adventure! During your adventure you will be able to choose different routes and make different decisions to affect the stats of your character! The three stats are Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence. These stats will be trained as you make decisions and will be tested at different bosses! There are times where every choice is a "correct" choice, while sometimes all answers result in failure depending on your previous choices. This is intentional, better luck next time! When you collect all of the items from the bosses you win! Have fun!
